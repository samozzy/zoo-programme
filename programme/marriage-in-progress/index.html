We know this is too long – we will edit. Marriage in Progress playfully sends up lifelong commitment while celebrating it – and critiques the vanity of performance while revelling in it. Lauren Katz (Curb Your Enthusiasm) and Joey Slotnick (The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel, The Good Wife) explore marriage as improvisation and improvisation as marriage. The Chicago Tribune called ED Ensemble’s ‘wonderfully energetic and self-effacing style’ an ‘adventurous and delightful experience’ and a ‘hilariously engaging’ and ‘surreal success.’ They emerged in Chicago as one of the most innovative companies in North America.
