Well, hello there! How do you Boo?? Teenage playwright Jaz Skingle brings her sell out debut play, Ghost Therapy, to the Edinburgh Fringe. Starring Trenetta Jones as Dr Soul, this hilarious family comedy discovers a magical mix of ghostly characters as they share their post-life issues in search of a lasting peace. Richard Dinnick, Writer for Doctor Who commented, “This is an amazing job by Jaz. Comedy’s hard, but this had me laughing out loud. A talent to watch out for!”
