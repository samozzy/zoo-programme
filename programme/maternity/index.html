‘I’m still waiting for my child… Every man I meet, I think maybe that’s my son’. A documentary protest performance, based on a true story, gives stage to voiceless mothers. The show examines the experience of oppressed childbirth and exposes a dark affair from the 1950s about the kidnapping of immigrant children, whose fate is still unknown. Maternity searches for an emotional connection between those mothers and women from all over the world. Founded by Hana Vazana Gruwald, Ha’Frehot ensemble is a collective of women creators dealing with issues of ethnicity, feminism and activism.

Age Guidance: 14+
