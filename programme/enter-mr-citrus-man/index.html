Pat and Pete live happily in their tiny botanic bathroom, so happily they don’t remember any ambitions they’ve had in life… until the day Mr Citrus Man, a life guru from the Big City, moves into the rest of their home. A strange letter and a forgotten dream suddenly leads Pat on a dangerous journey, where what she loves most is on the line. Enter Mr Citrus Man is a philosophical tragicomedy about love and ambition that combines thigh-slapping humour with life’s big questions, leaving you with new thoughts on the emotions you thought you knew.

Age Guidance: 12+
