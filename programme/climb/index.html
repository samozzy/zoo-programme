Jamaican-Canadian singer-songwriter, Duane Forrest, shares his stories and songs gleaned from his travels around the world. Whether he’s singing about love found and lost in Latin America, or telling stories about seeking clarity gigging throughout Asia and Europe, his soulful voice and samba-reggae vibes seek joy and hope in this broken world. Proceeds support Duane’s charity Genesis Community of the Arts.
