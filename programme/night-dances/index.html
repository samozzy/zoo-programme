A series of dance poems expressing the invisible, energetic, connective tissue that joins us.

An assault on the senses and an ode to the body, all breathing the same air, blood, sweat and tears. Love, fury, pride sharing a moment.

Punch a hole into the future.

A deep groove for our eyes, ears and hearts. This one’s for all of us. The time for dancing has come.

May the salt from our sweat unite us.

Live music by Daniel Fox (Gilla Band).

‘Raucous, loud, sweaty… a thrilling hour of relentless dancing and music’ ★★★★ (Irish Times).

Presented in association with Dance Base. A co-production with Dublin Theatre Festival and supported by Arts Council of Ireland and Culture Ireland.
