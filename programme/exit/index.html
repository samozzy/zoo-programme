Four circus artists move through a shifting world of revolving walls and floating doors.

In EXIT, choreographer Piet Van Dycke blends circus and dance into a thrilling physical performance where movement, partner acrobatics and balance form one shared language. With backgrounds in bascule, aerial belts, trapeze and acro-dance, they create a dynamic interplay of trust, risk and transformation. They propel, catch, climb and balance as they search for their own path, yet need one another to keep moving.

A powerful, visually striking exploration of independence, connection and fragile balance.

Four bodies. One path. No way out alone.

Age Guidance: 8+
