Look into the human face of greed – live acting, visuals and a binaural soundscape that gives you the chills. The cum-ex tax scam: 50 billion GBP ‘robbed’ from the treasuries around Europe! The use of headphones and 3D-sound creates an intimate connection with the cynical financier on stage when he is interrogated, at a party in the club, in the shower, in a bank in Zürich. ‘Gifted, socially engaged performance’ ***** (Kulturkupeen.dk) ’Innovative and exciting’ ***** (Scenekanten.com). ‘Mindful and sensual’ ***** (Ungt Teaterblod).
