Accompanied by a glittering live band and fresh from two sold-out London and Cambridge runs, don’t miss the Fringe premiere of Ed, charting the story of a ginger pop sensation. Can Ed deal with fame, fortune and being ginger?

Starring multiple authentic gingers and featuring Rosie Parrish as Ed. ‘A reminder of the comedy songs of Bo Burnham … Hilarious lyrics … Instantly in stitches’ (Varsity newspaper).

A cheeky and raucous night out, championing auburn culture.

Content Warnings

  • Audience participation
  • Strong language/swearing
