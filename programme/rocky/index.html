Award-winning political theatre about the harmless, loser boxer Rocky, who against all odds defeats his own inferiority and unreasonable loser life. He has all the wrong opinions, though fortunately no airtime. But what happens when he breaks character and gains power? Rocky! is fiercely entertaining and will swipe you off your feet. ‘Left me speechless and with a hammering heart’ ★★★★★ (Scenekanten.com). ‘This experience proved to me that theatre is able to deal with the most problematic discussions of zeitgeist and power’ ★★★★★ (Berlingske).

Part of #DANISH showcase at Edinburgh Fringe 2022.

Age Guidance: 12+
