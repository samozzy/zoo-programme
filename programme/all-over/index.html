A poetic, subtle and witty dance performance on conventions, expectations and perception. As in an audio guide, or a fake indoctrination machine, every audience member is presented with individual text through headphones – hanging from the ceiling. The project seeks to emphasize the notion of being singular/plural through an interpassive system of textual, auditive and bodily movement material. As in a simulator of the imaginary, three dancers take your metaphorical hand and guide you through your own imagination.

Age Guidance: 16+
