Ever felt like giving up? Ever wondered how some people keep going, no matter what? With cruise ships, prosthetic body parts, angry opticians and the best ending ever, Aidan (as seen on BBC One and at the Hammersmith Apollo) guides us through a tale that leads to dreams coming true. ‘It’s heart-warming positive comedy, like no one else is doing, and the world really needs it right now’ (Romesh Ranganathan). ‘Goatley is fabulous, funny, warm and tingly’ (Mark Thomas). ‘Hilarious’ (BroadwayBaby.com). ‘Brilliant’ ★★★★★ (FringeGuru.com).
