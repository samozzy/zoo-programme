The Best Man Show is an interactive and darkly hilarious wedding reception where comedian Mark Vigeant plays the Groom’s brother Paul, who has been asked to give the toast at an untraditional polyamorous commitment ceremony.

It starts out fun and silly, with your typical masculine roasting and ribbing – and slowly devolves into a drunken existential crisis where Paul tries desperately to understand what it means to love, but can’t get over his own masculinity to recognize how lonely he is.

Filled with laughter, singing, dancing, crying, screaming, clowning, bubbles, audience participation, and more.

Age Guidance: 14+

Content Warnings

  • Audience participation
  • Strong language / swearing
