‘He’s seen my Foo Foo. He’s seen my cave of wonders. The hidden but not-so-lost city of Atlantis. I might need to get another therapist.’ Nympho is trapped in the nostalgia of the 00s, afraid to confront her sexual past and the havoc it’s unleashing on her life. Sex therapy, dildos and a booty call named Jake can’t save her now. Martha May Markham’s debut play unleashes itself on the Fringe this summer. Hold onto your pants, it’s gonna be a bumpy ride.
