This simple question, made me dig deep and transported me to places I never imagined.

What this means in my country - India - is not a simple answer.

I decided to search on my own and not blindly believe what the scholars were telling us. I wanted first-hand information!

Why is there so much importance given to this language in a non-English-speaking nation?

Will I find the answers I’m looking for?

Join my journey of revelations, through theatre and contemporary dance.

Don’t miss out on this unforgettable experience that promises to captivate, provoke, and inspire!
