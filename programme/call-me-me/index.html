Maryellen barrells through fiercely feminist and seriously silly stand-up and storytelling littered with laughs (an aliteration).

Stay on the edge of your seat through stories of medical malpractice, being pigeon-toed, mansplaining in escape rooms, enjoying gaytimes (the icecream and the passtime), and spin cycling with Saoirse Ronan.

With a brother in the world-famous Grammy-award winning band ‘Rüfüs Dü Söl’, if he’s good at the music, ME must be good at the comedy… right?

Is that cocky? Yes, you can be cocky without one ;)
