‘There is truly nobody out there like Sh!t Theatre’ (Time Out).

The greatest story never told of the first ever female president of a country in the history of the world… whose name is… um… er…

Two time Fringe First winners Sh!t Theatre travel across Argentina and Spain chasing the story of the record-breaking president, endeavouring to write a musical to match Tim and Andrew’s. Truth! Justice! Songs! Wizards! And, if you insist, naked roller-skating.
