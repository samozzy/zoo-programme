Award-winning contemporary circus company returns with an extraordinary show looking at the hectic pace of today’s world. In a unique set design featuring a giant treadmill, four performers and two musicians mix dance, running, cyr-wheel acrobatics and original live music, while running almost a whole marathon. This UK debut for Runners marks the company’s fourth Edinburgh appearance following sell-out seasons in 2011, 2015 and 2017. Best Czech Theatre 2020 and 2021 (Theatre Critics Awards). Total Theatre Award nominee. ‘No wonder this low-fi, anarchic circus have been hoovering up awards wherever they stumble’ (Scotland on Sunday).

Age Guidance: 7+
