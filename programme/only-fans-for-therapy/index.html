OnlyFans for Therapy is a darkly comic solo about trauma, accountability and the absurd economics of healing.

Whilst triggered on holiday, a woman realises the only way to remain a functional member of society is therapy for life. Quick maths: 2,599 sessions at £70 each. And that’s without inflation. So who pays: the victim, the perpetrator or the state?

A razor-edged exploration of abuse, revenge and survival in a world where recovery comes with a receipt.

‘An exceptionally strong and intelligent performance, full of humour’ (E-tcetera.be).

Part of Big in Belgium.

Age Guidance: 14+

Content Warnings
