Come and enjoy a night in celebration of one of the world’s most beloved entertainers, a one Ms Judy Garland. My Best Judys features world-class entertainers featuring only the best tunes from the titans of the American songbook, Harold Arlen and George Gershwin amongst others. Starring vocalists Caitlin Cassidy, Dani Sicari and Madeleine Joyce. This show received rave reviews at Fringe World Festival in Western Australia were it was first premiered and is now coming to the UK for EdFringe.
