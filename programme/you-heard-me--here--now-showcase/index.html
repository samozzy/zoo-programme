You Heard Me is for anyone who has been told to shut up. You Heard Me takes up space and refuses to be silenced. The story of one woman’s power to re-make, re- mould and disrupt. This is for anyone who has been underestimated. Integrating a blend of visceral movement, enveloping sound and lights, Luca and her collaborators involve and immerse us in the messiness and effort of connecting to your own power. This show exists because without using her voice, Luca wouldn’t. This is a loud show about quiet power.

‘You Heard Me’ is part of Here & Now - Performance created in England. Here & Now is one of four showcase programmes of work from the four UK nations at the Edinburgh Festivals. Here & Now selects exceptional, stand-out performance created in England which is ready for international distribution.

You Heard Me is an ARC Stockton Production, co-commissioned by The Albany, BAC, Cambridge Junction, Northern Stage and Theatre in the Mill.
