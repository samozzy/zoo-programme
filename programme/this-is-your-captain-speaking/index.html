After years of patching up a rapidly deteriorating airport on an island lost in a Foie Gras scandal, Lick is staring down the propeller of a cargo plane. Fake weddings, burning bridges, mystery tickets, aerial acrobatics and flightless geese. The grounded passengers struggle to keep the building alive long enough to decide where to go next. There are five actors, five characters and 120 possible ways the show could go, promising a different performance each night. The cast know as much as the audience when they step on the stage.
