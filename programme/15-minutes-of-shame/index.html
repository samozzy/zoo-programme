P﻿lunge into the desperate world of a young London couple facing the brutal reality of financial struggle. Tired of watching others’ curated success online and frustrated by their own limited social media reach, they decide to chase the elusive dream of fame and fortune by launching an OnlyFans account. But as subscribers grow, their connection in reality drops.

Balancing biting comedy with raw drama, this provocative and unflinching mirror to our times tracks the high costs of monetising intimacy.
